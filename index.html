Dear Izzah,

Thank you for being someone so special in my life. You make my days brighter, my heart softer, and my life happier. I am truly grateful for every moment we've shared together. From the very first time we started talking, I had a feeling that you were someone I wanted to build a future with, grow old with, and create countless beautiful memories together.

No matter what happens, whether it's disagreements, misunderstandings, or difficult times, I want you to know that I will always stay by your side. Through every smile and every challenge, you will always be my special person, and I will never stop choosing you. 💗

Lily of the Valley is your favorite flower, and every time I see it, it reminds me of you. It reminds me of your gentle heart, your kindness, and the happiness you bring into my life.

I'm sorry for the times I've disappointed you, made you cry, or made you feel anything less than how truly loved you are. You deserve so much better, and I promise that I'll continue learning, growing, and becoming a better person for us.

I hope this little gesture brings a smile to your face because you deserve all the happiness, all the love, and every beautiful thing this world has to offer.

Thank you for loving me, for believing in me, and for staying with me through everything.

I love you so much, today, tomorrow, and always. 💗
